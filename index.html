An Atlas of US Honey Industry

This is a storytelling website that helps people investigate whether and how the decline occurred in recent years, as well as the response to the decline. The narrative is developed with the help of a series of interactive maps and statistical graphs. It allows users to explore both the temporal trends and the spatial patterns of US bee colonies, honey production, honey yield, colony loss, honey price, and international honey trade.
100yr Precipitation Event (in) and Extreme Events during 2010-2020

It is a map of Wisconsin’s precipitation created for the Nelson Institute Issue Brief. The base layer is the contour polygons of 100-year precipitation event across the state. The data were converted from a NetCDF file. The top layer is the dots of extreme rainfall events that exceeded the 100-year threshold. For the map style, I was asked to imitate the example given by the client.
